== Description ==

Swiper gives you the ability to embed Tinder style swipable cards with content from your posts or pages. Users can swipe through these cards, left to 'dislike', or right to 'like'. Liked items will then be saved in a list below.

[Upgrade to the PRO edition](https://www.southdevondigital.com/shop/ "Upgrade to the PRO edition") to unlock support for WooCommerce products, BuddyPress Profiles, and extra premade styles. You also get one year of free updates & priority support with your license.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Use the Swiper admin screen to generate a shortcode for your content and style
1. Add the shortcode to a page or post where you want your swipe cards to appear
